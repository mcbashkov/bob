How To buy

1

create A WALLET

Download the Phantom extension from the official website and install it. Fund it by transferring SOL from an exchange.

2

connect to dex

Connect to a decentralized exchange (DEX) Raydium. Open the DEX website and click on "Connect Wallet".Then select Phantom

3

swap sol for $btc

To swap sol for $btc, select sol in the "from" field and choose $BTc in the "to" field using our contract address. Enter the amount of tokens you want & press swap.

4

manage tokens

After swapping SOL for $BTc, simply wait, and the $BTc tokens will automatically appear in your Phantom Wallet. The process may take a few moments,
